The art of interacting incorrectly

Animation, Website, Poster

interactingincorrectly.online

The Art of Interacting Incorrectly challenges seamless interactions with technology by subverting its uses.

Through the creation of an animation using unconventional methods, along with an accompanying website, the project explores how disrupting technology can encourage more thoughtful and engaged interactions.

Creating Imperfection

Typefaces, Zine

Creating Imperfection explores the positive side of imperfection through the creation of 16 typefaces and a zine.

Some of the typefaces were created using worn, textured letters taken from found typography, and others were hand-written and distorted in various ways.
